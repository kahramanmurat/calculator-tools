Power Formulas

  • P = V × I (Power = Voltage × Current)
  • P = I² × R (Power = Current² × Resistance)
  • P = V² / R (Power = Voltage² / Resistance)

Common Power Units

1 kilowatt (kW) = 1,000 watts

1 megawatt (MW) = 1,000,000 watts

1 horsepower (hp) ≈ 746 watts
