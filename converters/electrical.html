About Electrical Units

Power (W, kW): Rate of energy consumption or production

Current (A): Flow of electric charge

Voltage (V): Electric potential difference

Resistance (Ω): Opposition to current flow

Capacitance (F): Ability to store electric charge

dBm/dBW: Power levels in decibels (logarithmic scale)

kVA: Apparent power (combination of real and reactive power)
