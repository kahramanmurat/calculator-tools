## üíµ Method 1: Display Advertising (Easiest to Start)

### Google AdSense
**Best for: Beginners, Quick Setup**

**How it works:**
- Add Google ads to your pages
- Get paid per click (CPC) or per impression (CPM)
- Calculator niches pay well: $2-10 per 1000 views

**Setup Steps:**
1. Apply at: https://www.google.com/adsense
2. Add ad code to your HTML pages
3. Place ads strategically:
   - Below calculator results
   - Sidebar (desktop)
   - Between content sections

**Expected Revenue:**
- 10,000 visitors/month = $50-200/month
- 50,000 visitors/month = $250-1,000/month
- 100,000 visitors/month = $500-2,000/month

**Where to Place Ads:**
```html
<!-- After calculator result box -->
<div id="result" class="result-box">
  <!-- Calculator result here -->
</div>

<!-- Ad placement -->
<div style="margin: 30px 0;">
  <!-- Google AdSense code here -->
</div>
```

### Alternative Ad Networks:
- **Ezoic** (Better rates than AdSense, requires 10K visits/month)
- **Mediavine** (Premium rates, requires 50K sessions/month)
- **AdThrive** (Highest rates, requires 100K pageviews/month)
