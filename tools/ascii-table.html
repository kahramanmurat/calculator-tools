About ASCII

ASCII (American Standard Code for Information Interchange) is a character encoding standard for electronic communication.

Range: 0-127 (7-bit) or 0-255 (8-bit extended ASCII)

Structure:

  • 0-31: Control characters (non-printable)
  • 32-47: Special characters and punctuation
  • 48-57: Numbers 0-9
  • 58-64: Special characters
  • 65-90: Uppercase letters A-Z
  • 91-96: Special characters
  • 97-122: Lowercase letters a-z
  • 123-126: Special characters
  • 127: DEL (delete)

Common ASCII Values

  • Space: 32
  • 0-9: 48-57
  • A-Z: 65-90
  • a-z: 97-122
  • Enter: 10 (Line Feed) or 13 (Carriage Return)
  • Tab: 9
  • ESC: 27
